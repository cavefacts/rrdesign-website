Options

Four heights — one craft

Available Heights

Every stool is available in four heights to suit a range of uses — from bar counters to fireside seating. All share the same handcrafted construction and tung oil finish.

30"

For use at bar height counters — typically those measuring 40″ to 42″. An elegant perch for kitchen islands and breakfast bars.

25"

The original Randall Ricketts stool. Suitable for occasional seating in a family room or around a kitchen island. The perfect height for guitar playing.

18"

Standard chair height. Ideal for use at a dining table or desk, where comfort and proportion matter most.

14"

A low, approachable piece — good for putting on your shoes or relaxing beside a fireplace on a slow afternoon.

Custom heights and finishes may be available — contact Randall to discuss your project.
